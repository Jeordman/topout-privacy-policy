Privacy Policy

Topout is a climbing training tracker for iOS. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Data We Collect

Topout does not collect personal information. All data you enter — climbing sessions, grades, sets, projects, and training notes — is stored exclusively on your device using SQLite. No data is ever transmitted to external servers, and Topout has no backend infrastructure or user accounts.

2. Calendar Access

Topout may request access to your device calendar so you can optionally add logged climbing sessions as calendar events. This permission is entirely optional. If granted, calendar data is used only to write events you explicitly create inside the app. No calendar data is read beyond what is needed to write those events, and nothing is stored outside your device.

3. Notifications

Topout may request permission to send local notifications for optional training reminders. These notifications are scheduled entirely on-device. No notification content or schedule is transmitted off your device.

4. Local Storage

All app data is stored locally in an on-device SQLite database. This data remains on your device and is subject to your device's standard backup settings (iCloud Backup, if enabled by you). You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling Topout.

5. Third-Party Services

Topout does not integrate any third-party analytics, crash-reporting, advertising, or data-collection SDKs. No data is shared with, sold to, or accessible by any third party.

6. Children's Privacy

Topout does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Because no data leaves the device, no personal information is collected from any user regardless of age.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
